THE AGE OF ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

  • INTRODUCTION

    • Artificial intelligence (AI) is defined as intelligence exhibited by an artificial entity. Such a system is generally assumed to be a computer. Although AI has a strong science fiction connotation, it forms a vital branch of computer science, dealing with intelligent behavior, learning, and adaptation in machines. AI systems are now in routine use in economics, medicine, engineering and the military as well as being built into many common home computer software applications, and traditional strategy games like computer chess and other video games.

  • HOW DOES ARTFICIAL INTELLIGENCE WORK

    • In the field of AI, it is important to understand how it works. AI is the process of creating computer programs that can solve problems. AI works by processing data and making decisions based on the data. It is possible to use AI to make transactions, monitor weather conditions, and tasks that could be difficult for humans to do. AI has been used for centuries to make some languages, data analysis and machine learning. Several tools are used, and techniques are used in machine learning, Including deep learning.
